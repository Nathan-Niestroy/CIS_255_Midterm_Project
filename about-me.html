# About Me

Hello! I'm currently pursuing my bachelor's degree with a focus on Computer Information Systems and additional minors in Cybersecurity and General Business at Saginaw Valley State University (SVSU). This academic foundation equips me with a versatile skill set in IT and cybersecurity, adaptable to various industries.

My exceptional organizational skills enable me to effectively manage my academic commitments while excelling in both part-time and full-time employment. With diverse professional experiences spanning education IT, healthcare IT, law enforcement cybersecurity, and various IT & cybersecurity projects, I've honed my problem-solving abilities and showcased my proficiency in delivering swift, precise solutions to technology challenges.

Before beginning my university studies, I had the privilege of attending an early college during my time as a high school student. This experience not only enriched my academic background but also allowed me to achieve my associate's degree at my local Community College while concurrently earning my high school diploma.

I'm committed to finding solutions and driven by a passion for assisting others, poised to deliver impactful contributions in any setting. My unwavering commitment to delivering exceptional customer service and leveraging my skills to assist others drives me to consistently pursue excellence in every aspect of my journey in the professional field and beyond.
